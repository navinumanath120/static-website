About Me

Hello! My name is Sudev, and I'm thrilled to share a bit about myself with you. I from the picturesque town of Thalassery in Kerala, where I've honed my skills as an artist specializing in portraits and mural paintings. Art has always been my passion, and I love capturing the beauty and emotions of my subjects through my work. Currently, I'm expanding my creative journey by studying cinematography and animation, blending traditional artistry with modern techniques to explore new dimensions of visual storytelling. I’m excited to connect with fellow art enthusiasts and explore how we can inspire each other through creativity.
